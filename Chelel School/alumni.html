Welcome to the Old Chelelians Portal

Turi Alumni are part of the international Chelel family. This area of our website allows Old Chelelians to connect with old friends, find out latest news and upcoming reunions. We are very grateful for the ongoing support and contribution of the Old Chelelians.

How it Works

All Chelelian students become members of the Old Chelelian Association the minute they depart for pastures new. This means they are welcome back at Chelel, at the Old Chelelian events, and can receive regular timely updates on what’s going on at the School. At present, the Old Chelelian database is being updated, and some of the details we hold might be out of date. Please help us keep it accurate by letting us know if you change your contact details.

Why not get in touch

If you are a former Old Chelelian student and haven’t been contacted recently, then please let us know your contact by getting in touch via either email: alumni@turimail.co.ke or call Judith Ndirangu on +254701799790.

You can always update your details by clicking here.

Keeping in Touch

We are always interested in receiving news about Old Chelelians. Let us know about where you moved to, married, and emigrated, your career path, family, and special interests. Are you in regular contact with another Old Chelelian? Encourage them to keep in touch. Do you have an interesting tale to tell about your time at School? You never know, you could be reading about your life in our next update on our website.

Connecting with Other Old Chelelians

Finally dear friends, I must remind you that we do not send addresses of third parties on request, for confidentiality reasons. We can assist by forwarding your contact details to the third party and letting them know that you would like to get in touch.
