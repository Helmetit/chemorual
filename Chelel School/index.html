Welcome to Chelel Memorial Schools

Established in 2020, Chelel Memorial Schools is a private institution in Nairobi, Kenya, pioneering exceptional modern education as the No.1 British Curriculum School in East Africa. Our students, aged 1 to 18, gain unrivaled independent day education across two exquisite campuses in the leafy suburbs of Karen and Runda, with premium boarding provisions at the Karen campus. At Chelel Memorial Schools, children achieve academic excellence with internationally recognized courses and complementary activities to develop their talents. Every child is unique, and our experienced educators apply a personalized teaching approach to meet each individual's needs. Our child-centered methodology yields outstanding results, preparing students for lifelong success.

Our International Schools in Nairobi

Chelel Memorial Schools proudly educates our bright, young learners with a British Curriculum at two expansive sites in the charming regions of Runda and Karen in the heart of Nairobi. Premium boarding is available at our Karen campus.

Why Choose Our International School in Nairobi, Kenya?

Chelel Memorial Schools provides a character-focused, values-driven education through a school-wide Global Citizenship program.

As a Round Square school, we promote experiential learning opportunities for all students through local, regional, and international experiences and excursions.

Student leadership opportunities are encouraged in our community to ensure our graduates are well-prepared to thrive with humility and an appreciation of social responsibility.

  • British National Curriculum School: The No. 1 British National Curriculum School in East Africa, offering a broad, creative, and unique future-focused curriculum.
  • Best IGCSE and A-Level Results: The best IGCSE and A-Level results in East Africa (2022).
  • Multicultural International School: A welcoming, multicultural international day and boarding school with over 50 nationalities.
  • Premium Boarding School: Premium boarding school in East Africa at our Karen campus.

Expanding the Bounds of Learning with Virtual Reality

Our classrooms are no longer static entities confined by four walls. At Chelel Memorial Schools, through the amalgamation of VR and the Metaverse, we're creating dynamic, interactive ecosystems where subjects come alive, inviting students to explore, question, and understand in ways previously unimaginable. VR-based sessions redefine the boundaries of learning, making it an experiential journey rather than a passive acquisition of information.
